The Best Movies According to Shea

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Birdy

Birdy is a rare and rewarding film, certain to be cherished by filmgoers seeking an alternative to the standard formulas.

Django Unchained

Bold, bloody, and stylistically daring.

Kill Bill Vol.1

The cast really blew me away, each and every actor did a great job with their respective roles.
